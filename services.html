Nutritional Medicine Consultations

In our nutritional medicine consultations, we use evidence-based research to assist in our case taking and to develop individually tailored nutritional prescriptions for our clients. A focus on your nervous system, gut health, sleep patterns, exercise and diet will all be taken into consideration.

Our initial consultations are 75 minutes long and include a thorough case taking of your presenting symptom(s), a diet analysis and may include some physical examinations, such as taking your blood pressure or a body composition analysis. At the end of the consultation, you will receive a nutritional prescription which may include practitioner only nutritional supplementation, dietary modifications (food as medicine), and lifestyle advice.

Note: Due to Covid-19 Straight Up Nutrition is only offering Telehealth consultations at this time through secure encrypted video conferencing.

General Wellbeing

Feeling a little sluggish or maybe your mood is a little low? A general wellbeing program is tailored to your needs and can help put a little pep in your step! Whether you want to increase your vitality through food as medicine or utilise nutritional supplementation to help you through a stressful life event, we're here to help. We can also order functional pathology testing such as fructose breath testing or gut microbiome mapping to improve treatment outcomes.

Love Your Guts

Poor gut health has been linked to low immunity, low mood, increased inflammation and even obesity. Love your guts is a four-week program aimed to heal and seal the gut. Followed by a recolonisation of healthy bacteria such as Lactobacilli and Bifidobacteria. If you currently experience ongoing flatulence, bloating, burping, reflux, indigestion, constipation, diarrhea, or undigested food in your stools this program is a must.

Preconception Boot Camp

Did you know that preconception in men is just as important as it is in women? This program is designed to help both partners achieve maximum wellness in preparation to conceive. It is preferable for both partners to start preconception at least three months before trying for a baby. This program includes detoxifying the body and your environment, as well as dietary advice and nutritional supplementation to ensure the best genetic material is on offer from both partners.

Eating for Pregnancy

It is likely that once you fall pregnant you will get nutrition advice from your GP, obstetrician or midwife, which is a great starting place. However, if you are looking for more specific advice on how to achieve your daily recommended intakes of iron, zinc, protein, DHA, etc... during each trimester of pregnancy look no further. We will offer you specific nutrition advice for each trimester of pregnancy and recommend high quality practitioner only nutritional supplements to ensure your little one is getting the best start!

Today I Ate A Rainbow

This is a specialised program for fussy eaters. It aims to introduce fruits and vegetables to children in a way which empowers them to try new foods and feel great about their choices! This program can also be taught to children in a group setting such as Early Child Care Centres or Kindergartens. Please get in touch if you would like to find out more information for group bookings.
